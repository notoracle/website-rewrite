I’m an A11Y advocate and also a designer. Currently, I’m a hobbyist building a Portfolio.

A11Y should be at the forefront of all design. However, when an afterthought, A11Y tends to be weak. In my work, A11Y is always a priority—the more accessible your design, the more people will use and share your work.

There’s always room for improvement, and nothing is ever complete. I’m open-minded and always eager to learn.
